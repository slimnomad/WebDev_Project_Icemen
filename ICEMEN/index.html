Crazy road

Lucas shot down the forbidden ridge, snow spraying like sparks. A hidden rock launched him into the air—too high, too far. For one breathless second he thought he was done... then his board stemmed down, wobbling but holding. Heart racing, he laughed into the wind. He hadn't just survived—he'd conquered the mountain.

EDGE OF THE SUMMIT

The trail ended, but Lucas kept climbing. The mountain narrowed into a razor-thin ledge, cliffs yawning on both sides. A loose stone gave way—his boot slipped. For a heartbeat, the world tilted, nothing but air below. He pressed against the rock, breath ragged, then pulled himself back up. When he reached the summit, the sky split open in gold, and he smiled. He hadn't just climbed a mountain—he'd stared into the void and kept going.
